Dussehra or Vijayadashmi - Why Do We Celebrate It ?

Dussehra - The 10th Day Of Celebration

Dussehra is a festival in India that signifies the victory of the Hindu Rohini family over their enemies, the Dharmashasas. It is celebrated on the 10th day of the lunar month of Makara (February) in the year 2021.

Vijayadashmi - The Day Of Victory

Vijayadashmi is celebrated on the 10th day of the lunar month of Rohini (March) in the year 2021. It is considered a day of pride and victory for the victory of the Rohini family over their enemies, the Dharmashasas.

Dussehra - Devotion & Reverence

Dussehra is a significant religious festival that has been celebrated for centuries. It is held in honor of the Rohini family, who were the first Hindu dynasty to conquer India. The festival is a time for devotion, pride, and celebration of the victory of the Hindu Rohini family over their enemies, the Dharmashasas.

Celebrate Dussehra With Joy & Love

Dussehra is a time of great joy and love for the Hindu Rohini family. The festival encourages people to remember their victory and celebrate it with love, pride, and devotion. By celebrating Dussehra, you can create a strong connection with your loved ones and help them feel proud of their achievements.
